BTB Capital Website
Marketing website for BTB Capital Pty Ltd, a Melbourne-based private peer-to-peer lending facilitator directed by George Loupos. BTB connects individual investors directly with specific loan opportunities — investors hold transparent positions in named loans, not pooled fund units.
Site Structure
Five-page architecture centred on BTB's service model and eight-step investor journey:

Home — Value proposition and audience entry points (investors, borrowers, brokers)
About Us — Team bios, company background, BTB's philosophy (headshots/bios pending from client)
Who We Work With — The three audiences BTB serves: investors, borrowers, and brokers
How It Works — Eight-step process from loan sourcing through to discharge, drawn from BTB's engagement letter
Get in Touch — Contact and enquiry form (CTA button in nav)

Key Design Principles

Service-oriented language — "we facilitate," "we manage," "we handle" — not product-pitch framing
Transparency as differentiator — investors see exactly which loans they're in, unlike pooled fund structures
Tone — switched-on energy, professional language, rare humour (per brand guidelines)
