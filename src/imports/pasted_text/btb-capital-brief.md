## 1. ABOUT THIS BRIEF

This is a standalone brief for a fresh website build in Figma Make. It represents a repositioned approach that accurately reflects BTB Capital as a **peer-to-peer private lending facilitator** — not a fund manager. The site is structured around the investor journey and BTB's hands-on service model, with copy drafted from the Director's own engagement letter to clients.

**Team:** 3 staff members
**Director:** George Loupos
**Legal Partner:** A&C Landmark (commercial and banking law)

**What BTB does:** BTB Capital acts as a personal representative for private investors in peer-to-peer lending. They source private funding opportunities, conduct due diligence, coordinate legal documentation through A&C Landmark, facilitate settlement, and manage the loan from start to finish — including default recovery. Every transaction is a direct, one-to-one arrangement between a specific investor and a specific borrower, secured by real property.

**What BTB is NOT:** BTB is not a managed investment fund. There is no pooled capital, no units, no PDS, no fund structure. The investor knows exactly where their money is, what it's secured against, and who the borrower is.

## 6. DESIGN NOTES

### Overall Aesthetic
Premium but approachable. Think trusted private advisor, not corporate financial institution. Clean layouts, generous white space, the blue palette should feel authoritative but warm when paired with the gold accents and Pale White backgrounds. Avoid stock photography of handshakes, skyscrapers, or generic "business" imagery. If imagery is used, Melbourne cityscapes or abstract textural backgrounds work best.

### Component Specifications

**Buttons:**
- Primary: Pale Gold (`#CBA981`) fill, Dark Blue (`#012746`) text, Montserrat Semi Bold, 4–6px border radius
- Secondary: Transparent with BTB Blue (`#0F3C60`) border and text
- Hover: Shift to Gold Text (`#A07E56`) fill

**Cards:**
- White background with subtle shadow on Pale White sections
- Thin Pale Gold (`#CBA981`) left-border or top-border accent
- Title: Montserrat Semi Bold, BTB Blue
- Body: Montserrat Regular, Dark Grey

**Section transitions:**
- Alternate between White, Pale White (`#F5F0EA`), BTB Blue (`#0F3C60`), and Dark Blue (`#012746`) backgrounds to create visual rhythm
- Use thin Pale Gold horizontal rules as dividers where sections share the same background colour

**Icons:**
- Line-style, not filled
- Pale Gold or White on dark backgrounds
- BTB Blue or BTB Light on light backgrounds

**Timeline/Steps (How It Works page):**
- Vertical layout on both desktop and mobile
- Numbered circles: BTB Blue fill, white number (Montserrat Semi Bold)
- Connecting line: Thin, Pale Gold
- Step label: Gold Text, all caps, tracked
- Step headline: Montserrat Semi Bold, BTB Blue
- Step body: Montserrat Regular, Dark Grey

### Responsive Behaviour
- Desktop-first with fully responsive mobile breakpoints
- Timeline stays vertical on all screen sizes
- Contact form goes full-width on mobile
- Navigation collapses to hamburger menu on mobile
- Footer stacks vertically on mobile
